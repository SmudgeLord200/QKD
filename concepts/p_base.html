Polarization Base/State

QKD uses TWO overall polarization bases throughout the transmission.

  1. Rectilinear Basis (Horizontal and Vertical)
  2. Diagonal Basis (+45º/-45º)

The photons pass through the photon detectors by randomly switching between the two polarization bases.

Rectilinear Diagonal

Horizontal

+45º

Vertical

-45º

It is the same when comes to encoding the photon, aka “qubits”,
such as bit 0 can be presented either as vertical state or -45º diagonal one;
bit 1 can be presented as either horizontal state or +45º diagonal one.
