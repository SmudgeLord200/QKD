Polarization Base/State

QKD uses TWO polarization bases:
Rectilinear (horizontal and vertical) and Diagonal basis (+45 º/-45º)
throughout the transmission.
The photons pass through the photon detectors by randomly switching between the two polarization bases.

Rectilinear Diagonal

Vertical

+45º

Horizontal

-45º

It is the same when comes to encoding the photon, aka “qubits”, such as bit 0 can be presented either as vertical state or -45º diagonal one; bit 1 can be presented as either horizontal state or +45º diagonal one.
